About NayePankh

Who We Are

NayePankh Foundation is a dedicated non-governmental organization committed to uplifting the marginalized sections of society. We believe in providing equal opportunities for everyone to grow and succeed.

Our Mission

To eradicate poverty and illiteracy by providing quality education and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Our Vision

A society where every individual has the resources and wings to achieve their dreams without discrimination.

Our Programs

Education Support

Providing free education, books, and scholarships to children from low-income families.

Skill Development

Vocational training programs for youth to enhance employability and foster entrepreneurship.

Community Outreach

Health camps, food distribution, and disaster relief programs for communities in need.

Women Empowerment

Supporting women through self-help groups, legal awareness, and financial independence initiatives.
